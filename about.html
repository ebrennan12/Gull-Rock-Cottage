ABOUT GULL ROCK COTTAGE

Gull Rock Cottage is nestled on the edge of the ocean overlooking Great Oyster Bay in Swansea, Tasmania, with direct access to the beach and stunning views of Freycinet and The Hazards. This 1970s stone cottage is charming, creative and cosy with a beautiful stone fireplace, wooden panelled walls and grand windows overlooking the ocean.

This 1860s stone cottage is charming, creative and cosy with a beautiful stone fireplace, wooden panelled walls and grand windows overlooking the ocean.

Fall asleep and wake to the sound of the waves gently crashing on the sand with beautiful ocean views from your bedroom. Relax and unwind on the beautifully sun-drenched deck and watch the gulls glide across the ocean and find their home on gull rock. Gaze at the clear, starry skies with a crisp Tasmanian wine in hand and swim or kayak in the sea.

Turn off, pause and escape at beautiful Gull Rock Cottage on the East Coast of Tasmania.
